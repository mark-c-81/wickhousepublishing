We're writers. We're readers.

Who is Wick House Publishing? We are a family-run, independent publisher. We heard a story that needed telling, in fact, we heard many stories that needed telling. Because we’re small, because we’re family, you’re family too. We want you to find your voice and let it be heard.


NICHOLAS DENENBERG

Creative Director

Nicholas can often be spotted in his mind-palace. If he isn’t constructing a limerick or verse, he is probably taking a photo of a mop or other humble, overlooked object. Nicholas is also a collector of many things, but mostly, people who need to be heard. He is the driving force behind Wick House Publishing.

JESSICA DENENBERG

Creative Director

Jessica has been reading since before she was in training pants. She is the mastermind and CEO of the family and can spot an error on a page faster than you can blink. Sometimes Nicholas is amazed at how many books she can get through in a week, making her the perfect editor. This type-A powerhouse will help you hone your work like a sculpture emerging from marble. Ready for a critic who will also be your champion? Look no further.
